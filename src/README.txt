Maker Space Manager READ ME

Maker Space Manager is a bespoke solution for site management of makerspaces
combining the fuctions of Customer Relationship Management, facility management,
credential management and scheduling.  

Please be aware the current version as of 04/27/17 is still an alpha version 
and not all functionality has been implemented.  

You can contact MakerSpaceManager@gmail.com for questions.
